About Us

Tribe Only is a multifaceted brand redefining innovation and convenience. What began as a tech company offering cutting-edge cybersecurity services, web development, product & motion design has evolved into a vibrant ecosystem of businesses.

Our Child Companies

Under Tribe Only, our child companies—Cyber Tribe, Easy Street, Tribe Foods, Tribe Prints, and Tribe Laundry—focus on creating accessible, high-quality solutions for university students and young professionals. From affordable meals to tailored laundry services down to fashion, our mission is to simplify everyday living while inspiring creativity and entrepreneurship.
